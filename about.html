AI & Computer Vision Engineer  |  Researcher

I'm an AI and Computer Vision engineer currently working at Deutsche Bank, where I apply machine learning and engineering at the intersection of Fintech and intelligent systems.

My research background sits at the interface of Cognitive Science and Machine Learning — particularly how insights from human perception can inform the design of more robust computer vision models. My Master's thesis focused on compression of 4D medical images, achieving an 18% improvement over NJPEG 2000.

I'm deeply interested in Healthcare AI — building tools that make clinical imaging workflows faster, smarter, and more accessible. I stay close to the frontier: LLMs, agentic pipelines, and large-scale vision models are spaces I actively build in.

I'm always working on something new — whether it's a research prototype, an open-source tool, or an experiment at the edge of what's possible.
